How to Correct Timezone?

Windows PC/Laptop

  1. Right-click the time on the taskbar.
  2. Left-click "Adjust Date/Time"
  3. Change "Set time automatically" to "On"
  4. Change "Time zone" to UTC+8:00 Taipei

Android Phone

  1. Go to your device settings.
  2. Search for "Date time"
  3. Change "Time zone" to GMT+8:00
